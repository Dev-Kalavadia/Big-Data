Title: Matthew Huang



Matthew Huang is a Canadian competitive swimmer.

At age 15, Huang qualified for the Canada National Swimming team. In 2000, he qualified as a member of the Canadian Olympic Swimming team to compete in Sydney, Australia.  Huang would go on to represent Canada at many international swimming competitions but only re-emerged in 2005 as a national team member to attend the World Aquatics Championships in Montreal and the World Student Games in Ä°zmir, Turkey.
