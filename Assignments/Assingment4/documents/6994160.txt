Title: Mozingo Lake

Mozingo Lake is a  reservoir in Nodaway County, Missouri on Mozingo Creek.

The reservoir is owned by the city of Maryville, Missouri and is the city's water source and operates as a 3,000 acre (12Â km2) park (including the Mozingo Lake Golf Course) with  of shoreline.

The reservoir was completed in 1994.  Water overflowed its dam in the Great Flood of 1993.
