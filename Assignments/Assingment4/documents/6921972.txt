Title: Mullet Key




Mullet Key is a historic island near Crystal River, Florida. It is located 3 miles south of the main mouth of the Crystal River, and was inhabited by Native Americans in pre-Columbian times. The island was occupied from roughly 500 to 1500 and was inhabited by the Deptford and Safety Harbor cultures. Oyster shell middens have been found at the site.Citrus County listings  at Florida's Office of Cultural and Historical Programs On July 3, 1986, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places.

==In popular culture==
Mullet Key is referenced in the movie The Punisher.
