Title: Carl Øyvind ApelandCarl Øyvind Apeland (also known as Calle Apeland) (born April 18, 1964). Is a Norwegian musician plays bass, guitar, and keyboard in the Norwegian band Vamp.Vamp in Store norske leksikon 

He has composed some of the band's hits like "Sommar i hekken", also co-wrote "Ingeborg" (with Jan Toft another band member), "Sirkus av lys", "Byen" and "Svin på skog" (with Odin Aarvik Staveland).
