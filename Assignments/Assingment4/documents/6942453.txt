Title: Saint Lucia Channel

Saint Lucia Channel is a strait in the Caribbean that separates French island Martinique, to the north,  and Saint Lucia, in the south. It is a pathway between Caribbean sea and Atlantic ocean.Ana G. López Martín International Straits: Concept, Classification and Rules of Passage. 218 pages. Publisher: Springer Science & Business Media, 2010   The Diamond Rock is in the St. Lucia Canal.

== History ==
On August 17, 2007, Hurricane Dean crossed the channel as a Category 2 storm.
