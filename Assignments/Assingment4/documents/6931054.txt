==Domestic career==
Clavier previously played for Portuguese side SC Freamunde and Angers SCO, helping them gain promotion from the Championnat National to Ligue 2 in the 2006–07 season.Championnat National final standings 2006–07

On 4 April 2012, Clavier signed a contract with V-League side, Dong Tam Long An FC.

==International career==
Clavier was called up to the Guadeloupe squad for the 2009 CONCACAF Gold Cup.
