Title: EzerNet



EzerNet Ltd. was a wireless and cable Internet service provider in Latvia, offering FTTH, MVDS, (pre-)WiMax and satellite access. In 2009 the company went bankrupt.http://www.lursoft.lv Lursoft
EzerNet was a holder of 42 GHz frequency licence in Latvia.http://www.sprk.gov.lv  Latvian Public Utilities Commission decision
