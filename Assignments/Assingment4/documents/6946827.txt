Title: Sanday Airport



Sanday Airport  is located  north northeast of Kirkwall Airport on Sanday, Orkney Islands, Scotland.

Sanday Aerodrome has a CAA Ordinary Licence (Number P541) that allows flights for the public transport of passengers or for flying instruction as authorised by the licensee (Orkney Islands Council). The aerodrome is not licensed for night use.Civil Aviation Authority Aerodrome Ordinary Licences
