Geča () is a village and municipality in Košice-okolie District in the Kosice Region of eastern Slovakia.

==History==
In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1255.

==Geography==
The village lies at an altitude of 185 metres and covers an area of 5.481 km2. It has a population of about 1500 people.

==Genealogical resources==

The records for genealogical research are available at the state archive "Statny Archiv in Kosice, Slovakia"
