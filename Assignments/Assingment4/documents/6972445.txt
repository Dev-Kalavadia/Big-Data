Title: Parlin, New Jersey



Parlin is an unincorporated community located within Old Bridge Township and Sayreville Borough in Middlesex County, New Jersey, United States.Locality Search, State of New Jersey. Accessed February 13, 2015. The area is served as United States Postal Service ZIP Code 08859.

As of the 2000 United States Census, the population for ZIP Code Tabulation Area (ZCTA) 08859 was 20,129.
