Title: Shigeharu Shiba is an anime audio director and producer. He graduated from Tokyo University of Education and went on to form the Jiyū Theatre Troupe. Shiba then joined Omnibus Promotion, where he worked on anime television series, OVAs, and movies. See Shiba Shigeharu at the Ghibli Data Base. Accessed September 10, 2006.
