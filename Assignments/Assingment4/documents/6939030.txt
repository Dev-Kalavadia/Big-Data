Title: George Buchheit

George Clifford BuchheitDuke University's Chanticleer, 1926 (March 22, 1898 – May 24, 1972) was an American college basketball coach.  He was the head of the Kentucky Wildcats men's basketball team of the University of Kentucky from 1920 to 1924, where he compiled a 44–27 record.  From 1924 to 1928, Buchheit was the head coach of the Duke Blue Devils men's basketball team of Duke University.

From 1932 to 1945, he was the head coach for the Bloomsburg University of Pennsylvania football team and compiled a 15–27–4 record.George C. Buchheit Records by Year , College Football Data Warehouse, retrieved February 6, 2009.

Buchheit attended University of Illinois.
