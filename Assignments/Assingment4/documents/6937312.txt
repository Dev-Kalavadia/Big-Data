Title: Tatoosh fire
The Tatoosh fire began in Washington on August 22, 2006.  It was sparked by lightning and in September 2006, it moved across the border into British Columbia.  As of September 10, 2006, it was still out of control and burning in E. C. Manning Provincial Park.
