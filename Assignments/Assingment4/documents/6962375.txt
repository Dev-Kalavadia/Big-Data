Title: Severus of Avranches


Severus of Avranches was born to a poor peasant family in France. According to historian Georges Goyau, Severus was a sixth century shepherd, "...who was perhaps Bishop of Avranches"  Goyau, Georges. "Coutances." The Catholic Encyclopedia Vol. 4. New York: Robert Appleton Company, 1908. 12 December 2021 

He was a shepherd in his youth. After joining the priesthood, he rose through the ranks of monk, priest and later abbot. He became bishop of Avranches. In his later years, he resigned his bishopric and returned to monastic life.
