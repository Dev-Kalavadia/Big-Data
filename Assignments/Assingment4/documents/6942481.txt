Title: Saint Vincent Passage
Saint Vincent Passage is a strait in the Caribbean, that separates the Saint Lucia and Saint Vincent.  It is a pathway between Caribbean sea and Atlantic ocean.Ana G. López Martín International Straits: Concept, Classification and Rules of Passage. 218 pages. Publisher: Springer Science & Business Media, 2010   Jorge Heine, Leslie F. Manigat  The Caribbean and World Politics: Cross Currents and Cleavages Pages 385 Publisher: Holmes & Meier, 1988   page 31
