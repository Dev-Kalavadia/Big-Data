Title: Tomáš Julínek

Tomáš Julínek (born 7 November 1956) is a Czech politician and physician. From 2006 to 2009, Julínek served as the Minister of Health of the Czech Republic. He is a member of the Civic Democratic Party.

==Career==
Julínek received his degree from Masaryk University in 1982. From 2003 to 2005, he worked on a reform of the health system of the Czech Republic. In September 2006, he was appointed Minister of Health of the Czech Republic replacing David Rath. Prime Minister Mirek Topolánek replaced him in January 2009 with Daniela Filipiová.Staff (13 January 2009) "Senátorka Filipiová přijala nabídku řídit zdravotnictví" Novinky (Czech news service), in Czech, accessed 24 January 2009
