Title: Pusp Jain




Pusp Jain (born 4 April 1956) was a member of the 13th Lok Sabha and 14th Lok Sabha of India. He represented the Pali constituency of Rajasthan and is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) political party.Many stalwarts, fewer candidates in RajasthanFourteenth Lok Sabha
