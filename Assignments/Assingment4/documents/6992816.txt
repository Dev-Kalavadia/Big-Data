Title: VezioVezio is a village and former municipality in the canton of Ticino, Switzerland.
thumb|Aerial view (1948)
In 2005 the municipality was merged with the other, neighboring municipalities Arosio, Breno, Fescoggia and Mugena to form a new and larger municipality Alto Malcantone.Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office  accessed 14 January 2010
