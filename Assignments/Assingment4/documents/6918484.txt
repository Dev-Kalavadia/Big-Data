Title: Stones River Confederate order of battleThe following Confederate States Army units and commanders fought in the Battle of Stones River of the American Civil War. The Union order of battle is listed separately. Order of battle compiled from the army organizationMultiple commander names indicate command succession of command during the battle or the campaign. during the campaign,Official Records, Series I, Volume XX, Part 1, pages 658-661 the casualty returnsOfficial Records, Series I, Volume XX, Part 1, pages 676-681 and the reports.Official Records, Series I, Volume XX, Part 1, pages 1093-1099

==Abbreviations used==

===Military rank===
* Gen = General
* LTG = Lieutenant General
* MG = Major General
* BG = Brigadier General
* Col = Colonel
* Ltc = Lieutenant Colonel
* Maj = Major
* Cpt = Captain
* Lt = Lieutenant

===Other===
* (w) = wounded
* (mw) = mortally wounded
* (k) = killed
* (c) = captured
