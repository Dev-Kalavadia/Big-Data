Title: The Eight Dimensions


The Eight Dimensions () is the third studio album by Taiwanese singer Jay Chou, released on 18 July 2002 by BMG Taiwan. JVR Music The Eight Dimensions album info 18 July 2002. Retrieved 2011-03-27

The album was nominated for five Golden Melody Awards. The album also won for an IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award for Best Selling Mandarin Album of the Year.IFPI Hong Kong 2002 IFPI Hong Kong Album Sales Awards winners list  Retrieved 2011-04-19

The tracks, "Secret Sign", "Back to the Past", and "The Final Battle", are listed at number 1, number 5, and number 42 respectively on the 2002's Hit FM Top 100 Singles of the Year chart. HITO Radio Hit Fm Annual Top 100 Singles Chart 2002 (#1 to 50) Retrieved 2011-04-16
