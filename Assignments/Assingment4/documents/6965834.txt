Title: Yelena Vinogradova
Yelena Vinogradova (, born 28 March 1964) is a female track and field athlete who represented the Soviet Union. She specialized in the 200 metres and often ran relay races, winning the silver medal in the 4 × 100 metres relay and placing eighth in the 200 m at the 1991 World Championships.Women 200m World Championship 1991 Tokyo (JPN) - Friday 30.08 . She won a gold medal in the women's 4 × 400 metres relay at the 1990 Goodwill Games.Athletics results. Goodwill Games. Retrieved on 2010-06-24.
