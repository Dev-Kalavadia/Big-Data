Title: Dvorníky-Včeláre

Dvorníky-Včeláre  () is a village and municipality in Košice-okolie District in the Kosice Region of eastern Slovakia.

==History==
In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1437.

==Geography==
The village lies at an altitude of 214 metres and covers an area of 13.632 km². It has a population of about 440 people.

==Genealogical resources==

The records for genealogical research are available at the state archive "Statny Archiv in Kosice, Slovakia"
