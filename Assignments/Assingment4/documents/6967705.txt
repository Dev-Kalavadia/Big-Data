Title: Yeh Hui-Mei


Yeh Hui-Mei () is the fourth studio album by Taiwanese singer Jay Chou, released on 29 July 2003 by Alfa Music. "Ye Hui Mei album info" JVR Music. 29 July 2003. Retrieved 2011-04-08 The album was named after his mother.

The album was nominated for eight Golden Melody Awards and won two awards, including Best Pop Vocal Album and Best Music Video for "Class 3-2". The album also won an IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award for Best Selling Mandarin Album of the Year. "2003 IFPI Hong Kong Album Sales Awards winners list" IFPI Hong Kong. Retrieved 2011-04-19

The tracks, "Sunny Day", "Her Eyelashes", and "East Wind Breaks", are listed at number 2, number 15, and number 41 respectively on the 2003's Hit FM Top 100 Singles of the Year chart. "Hit Fm Annual Top 100 Singles Chart 2003 (#1 to 50)" HITO Radio. Retrieved 2011-06-21
