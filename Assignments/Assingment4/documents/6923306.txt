Title: Magdalena District, Chachapoyas

Magdalena is a district of the province of Chachapoyas in the Amazonas Region of Peru.

==Geography==
Magdalena is located in the south of the Chachapoyas Province, in the high part of the inter-Andean valley of the river Utcubamba.
