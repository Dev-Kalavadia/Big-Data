Blažice () is a village and municipality in Košice-okolie District in the Kosice Region of eastern Slovakia.

==History==
Historically, the village was first mentioned in 1245.

==Geography==
The village lies at an altitude of 205 metres and covers an area of .
It has a population of about 500 people.
