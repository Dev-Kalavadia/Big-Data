Title: Christiane Krause






Christiane Krause (born 14 December 1950) is a German athlete who competed mainly in the 100 metres.

She competed for West Germany in the 1972 Summer Olympics held in Munich, Germany in the 4 x 100 metres where she won the gold medal with her teammates Ingrid Becker, Annegret Richter and Heide Rosendahl.Olympic results
