The Estadio Victoria de Girón is a multi-use stadium in Matanzas, Cuba.  It is currently used mostly for baseball games and is the home stadium of Matanzas Cocodrilos.  The stadium holds 22,000 and was built in 1977.www.worldstadiums.com
