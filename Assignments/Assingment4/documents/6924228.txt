Title: Pereiaslav-Khmelnytskyi Raion

Pereiaslav-Khmelnytskyi Raion () was a raion (district) in Kyiv Oblast of Ukraine. Its administrative center, Pereiaslav (from 1943 until 2019 the city was named Pereiaslav-Khmelnytskyi The council renamed Pereyaslav-Khmelnitsky, Ukrayinska Pravda (30 October 2019)), was incorporated separately as a town of oblast significance and did not belong to the raion. The raion was abolished on 18 July 2020 as part of the administrative reform of Ukraine, which reduced the number of raions of Kyiv Oblast to seven. The area of Pereiaslav-Khmelnytskyi Raion was merged into Boryspil Raion. The last estimate of the raion population was  .

At the time of disestablishment, the raion consisted of four hromadas, 
* Divychky rural hromada with the administration in the selo of Divychky;
* Studenyky rural hromada with the administration in the selo of Studenyky;
* Tashan rural hromada with the administration in the selo of Tashan;
* Tsybli rural hromada with the administration in the selo of Tsybli.
