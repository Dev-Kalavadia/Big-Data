Title: Tanjung Piandang
thumb|Mukim Tanjung Piandang in Kerian District

Tanjung Piandang (Chinese: 角头) is a small town and mukim in Kerian District, Perak, Malaysia.http://apps.water.gov.my/jpskomuniti/dokumen/Kerian_Profail_Mac_2011.pdf 

==Geography==
The mukim consists of 15,696 residents and spans over an area of 40.76 km2.

==Economy==
Its primary economic activities include fishing and paddy farming.
