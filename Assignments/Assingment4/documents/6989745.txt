Title: Kosa (sports manufacturer)Kosa is a Swedish sports equipment manufacturer, situated in Motala, Sweden and mainly concentrated on equipment for bandy. Kosa is one of the leading brands in the sport, making bandy sticks, bandy balls, and other equipment.Sportamore: Kosa, retrieved 29 April 2014Bandyshopen: Kosa , retrieved 29 April 2014Mersport: Kosa, retrieved 29 April 2014
