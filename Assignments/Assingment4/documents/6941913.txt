Title: Vincenz Hruby
Vincenc Hrubý (9 September 1856 – 16 July 1917, Trieste) was a Czech chess master.

He was born in Krivsoudov (Bohemia).logicalchess.com Hrubý worked as a teacher at a secondary school (Realschule) in Trieste. He died there as well.

His best results were in 1880s and 1890s.
