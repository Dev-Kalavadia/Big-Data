Title: ATM-74 mineThe ATM-74 is a North Korean wooden box mine, it is a copy of the Soviet TMD-B. It is a wooden box packed with blocks of TNT, the top of the mine has three planks of wood underneath the central plank is a pressure fuze similar to Soviet MV-5 pressure fuze.

==Specifications==
* Length: 320 mm
* Width: 290 mm
* Height: 160 mm
* Weight: 9 kg
* Explosive content: 6 kg of TNT
* Operating pressure: 200 to 500 kg
