Title: Patrick Redmond

Patrick Redmond (born 1966) is an English author of psychological thrillers; typical themes include insanity, secrets and death. He attended Felsted School, then studied law at Leicester University and British Columbia in Vancouver. Before becoming a writer, he worked for eight years as a solicitor in London.Patrick Richmond at Fantastic Fiction

==Bibliography==
