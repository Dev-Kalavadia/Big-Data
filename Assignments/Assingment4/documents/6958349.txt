Title: Giuseppe Alessi (footballer)




Giuseppe Alessi (born 29 December 1973) is a retired Italian footballer. 
Alessi started his career at Torino. Then he played for S.S.C. Napoli, before joining Spezia Calcio.

==Honours==
*Serie C1: 2006
*Serie C2: 2008
