Title: Jenn Forgie





Jennifer Forgie (born January 4, 1969) is a Canadian actress and singer, who is often credited as Jenn Forgie.

==Biography==

Forgie is well known for her anime roles on Inuyasha, as Jakotsu, the homosexual member of The Band of Seven and Ranma Â½ as Tsukasa. She also played May Kanker and Nazz von Bartonschmeer during the third season of the animated series Ed, Edd n Eddy. However, the creator, Danny Antonucci, preferred Nazz and May's previous voice, Erin Fitzgerald, and had her flown to Canada to replace her for future episodes. She has also appeared in a TV movie about Flight 93.
