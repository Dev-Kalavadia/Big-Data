Title: Jag ska aldrig lämna dig

Jag ska aldrig lämna dig is a 1993 studio album from Swedish country and pop singer Kikki Danielsson. The songwriters Mikael Wendt and Christer Lundh had travelled to India to get inspiration for the record.Kikki Danielsson - Kikkis bästa album lines - 2008.

==Svensktoppen==
*The ballad Som en sol from the album charted on Svensktoppen for seven weeks during the period August 28-October 9, 1993, where it peaked at #7.Svensktoppen - 1993
*The song Jag ska aldrig lämna dig charted on Svensktoppen for four weeks during the period June 4–25, 1994, where it peaked at #4.Svensktoppen - 1993
