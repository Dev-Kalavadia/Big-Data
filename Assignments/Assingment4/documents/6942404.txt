Title: Dominica Passage

Dominica Passage is a strait in the Caribbean. It separates the islands of Dominica, from Marie-Galante, Guadeloupe.Dominica Passage: Guadeloupe National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency, Bethesda, MD, USA  It is a pathway from the Caribbean Sea into the Atlantic Ocean.Ana G.
