Title: Hewitt, New Jersey
Hewitt is an unincorporated community located within West Milford Township in Passaic County, New Jersey, United States.Locality Search, State of New Jersey. Accessed June 9, 2016. The area is served as United States Postal Service ZIP Code 07421. It is named for Abram Hewitt.

==Demographics==


As of the 2010 United States Census, the population for ZIP Code Tabulation Area 07421 was 7,439.
