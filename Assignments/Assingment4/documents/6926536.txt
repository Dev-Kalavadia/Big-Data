Title: Model 52 mineThe Model 52 mine is a Swedish circular plywood cased anti-tank blast mine. It is in service with the Swedish army. The mine can be fitted with a number of different fuzes and pressure plates including a three pronged plate and a pentagonal pressure spider and a tilt-rod fuze.

==Specifications==
* Diameter: 345 mm
* Height: 77 mm
* Weight: 8.98 kg
* Explosive content: 7.48 kg of TNT
* Operating pressure: 250 kg or 14.5 kg tilt
