Title: Promise (Jagged Edge song)

"Promise" is a song by R&B group Jagged Edge. The song spent two weeks at number one on the U.S. R&B chart and peaked at number 9 on the U.S. Pop chart. It was number 7 on the Rhythmic Top 40.All Music Credits It was ranked number 48 on the 2001 Billboard Hot 100 Year End.
