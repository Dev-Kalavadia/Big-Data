Košický Klečenov (; ) is a village and municipality in Košice-okolie District in the Kosice Region of eastern Slovakia.

==History==
In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1427.

==Geography==
The village lies at an altitude of 324 metres and covers an area of 13.4 km². The municipality has a population of about 270 people.

==Genealogical resources==

The records for genealogical research are available at the state archive "Statny Archiv in Kosice, Slovakia"

* Roman Catholic church records (births/marriages/deaths): 1789-1918 (parish B)
* Greek Catholic church records (births/marriages/deaths): 1788-1912 (parish A)
